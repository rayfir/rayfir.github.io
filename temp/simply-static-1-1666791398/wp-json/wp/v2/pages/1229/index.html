\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n What is Rayfir? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Rayfir is collaborative people that work and thrive together using their passion for art & design as their fuel, determined to bring our creation to the world by creating online art projects that you need it. As we try to grow our community, please join our discord server so we can do more collaborative work.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n What is the purpose of Rayfir? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

In short, we try to create a place where “creators and people meet and work together” for hobbies or professional projects.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Can you explain more about the purpose? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

The longer version is that creating an art project is expensive for an artist especially if a design process is included, most days are exhausted for creating a concept before the execution, even so, there are many times that revision is needed to make the best. This is what makes most artists is failed to gain financial independence in their life. On other hand, most of the time to get the professional quality of art that you wanted you need to spend more. On Rayfir, we hope that more people join our community, and create art together with us. Discussing together what art you want and whether we could create it for you. In that way, if common ground is met, together with other people from the community, you can purchase art by request at its lowest cost but the highest possible quality.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Can I request any art? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Yes, but the kind of request we could accept will depend on our capability to work on it. You can join our discord server to discuss it. For sure, we prefer to create digital art, any kind from illustration, concept, design, 3d, etc. We are however still trying to grow our team, so there’s not much we can do at the moment.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Can I join you? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

By joining our discord server, you are already part of our community. We are however limiting our staff, to prevent disarray. If you are an art creator and have a different set of skills than what is already shown in our gallery, you can email us your portfolio, we will contact you to process further.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n How can we contact you? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

You can contact us on any social platform of your choice for better fast respond from one of our team or from community, the link is available on bottom page. If you think you have important matter to discuss to us like a business proposal, you can email us at “staff@rayfir.com”, please make sure that you write down your business in detail so we can understand what your need is properly.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n \t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>

Project and Service<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n What is Project on Rayfir? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Project is an art project that is created for the community or mass-produced art that everyone could get. You and the community request any art, we discuss it together and we create it for you. It may need to be purchased at a low cost or distributed for free.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n What is Service on Rayfir? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Service is an art service that is created for individuals and made by order. The closer term to the term is commission-based art. This however will not always be available.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Why it is called an art? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

From poems to videos to winery sometimes people call it art for a reason. Our project and service are called art because it is an expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, our works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Who worked for the project and service? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Our creator, which is part of our staff. Depending on what project or service you required we will give the specialist in the field. Both the type and style that you required will be considered when selecting the designer or artist for the project or service.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n What is the difference between original art vs fan art? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Original art is created and licensed by Rayfir, so it is 100% copyrighted by Rayfir. Fan art is an art that you or the community request but aren’t copyrighted by Rayfir. Original Art can become a commercial project while Fan Art cannot be a commercial project.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n \t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>
