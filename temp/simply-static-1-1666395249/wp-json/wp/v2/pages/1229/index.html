\r\n

Rayfir is collaborative people that work and thrive together using their passion in art & design as their fuel, determined to bring our creation to the world by creating online art project that you need it. As we try to grow our community, please join our discord server so we can do more collaborative work.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n What is the purpose of Rayfir? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

In short, we try to create a place at which “creator and people meet and work together” for hobby or professional project.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Can you explain more about the purpose? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

The longer version is that, creating art is expensive for an artist especially if design process is included, most day are exhausted for creating concept before the execution, even so, there’s many times that revision is needed to make the best. This is what make most artist is failed to gain financial independent in their life. On other hand, most of the time to get professional quality of art that you wanted you need to spend more. On Rayfir, we hope that more people join our community, and creating art together with us. Discussing together what art that you want and whether we could create it for you. In that way, if common ground is meet, together with other people from the community, you can purchase an art by request at its lowest cost but highest possible quality.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Can I request any art? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

Yes, but kind of request we could accept will depend on our capability to work on it. You can join our discord server to discuss about it. For sure, we prefer to create digital art, any kind from illustration, concept, design, 3d, etc. We are however still trying to grow our team, so there’s not much we can do at the moment.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n
\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Can I join you? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

By joining our discord server, you actually already part of our community. We are however limiting our staff, to prevent disarray. If you are art creator and having different set of skill than what is already shown in our gallery, you can email us your portfolio, we will contact you to process further.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n

\r\n

You can contact us on any social platform of your choice for better fast respond from one of our team or from community, the link is available on landing page, just click on one of the icons you recognize. If you think you have important matter to discuss to us like a business proposal, you can email us at “staff@rayfir.com”, please make sure that you write down your business in detail so we can understand what your need is properly.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n \t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>

\r\n

Rayfir project is an art project that is created for community or mass-produced art that everyone could get. You and community request any art, we discuss it together and we create it for you. It may need to be purchased at low cost or distributed for free.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n

Rayfir service is an art service that is created for individual and made by order. The closer term to the term is commission-based art. This however will not always available.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n\r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n <\/i> <\/span>\r\n \r\n <\/span>\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n Why it is called an art? <\/span>\r\n\r\n <\/div>\r\n
\r\n

From poem to video to winery sometimes people call it art for a reason. Our project and service are called art because it is expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, our works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

Our creator that is part of our staff. Depend on what project or service that you required we will give the specialist in the field. Both type and style that you required will be considered when selecting the designer or artist for project or service.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n

\r\n

Original art is created and licensed by Rayfir, so it is 100% copyrighted by Rayfir. Fan art is an art that you or community request but aren’t copyrighted by Rayfir. Original Art can become a commercial project while Fan Art cannot be commercial project.<\/p> <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n <\/div>\r\n \t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>
